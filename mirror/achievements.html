SOFTWARE PRODUCTS THAT I HAVE BUILT.

Deltrix: This is an online platform that enables schools to post
results of their students online, for easier access. Students get to have their personal portals
The platform has online links to career paths that they may like and helps in results analysis.

Start Date: 1st January 2021 Launch date: 20th March 2021 Personnel Involved: Me (I coded the entire platform)

NoLines: It enables autonomous checkout for shoppers in Super-markets thus,
removing the need of long lines at the counters.
The user downloads an app on their phone that enables them to connect to any preferred mobile payment service.
As they bag and shop, the software automatically bills them for products in the cart. We used A.I in computer vision
that made it possible for products to be immediately taken off the billing list, when the user returns it on the shelf.

Start Date: 1st January 2022
Launch Date: 28th September 2022
Team that built it: Hillary Okoth (backend developer responsible for API's that connect the phone to the supermarket's
online system and mobile money platforms)
Cymona Lalampaa: Frontend Developer and Head of UX and UI
Yasmin Abdi: A.I and Machine Learning Engineer: Engineered computer vision software and other necessary entities.

Current Status of the project: Sold it to TechnoBrain Ltd for an undisclosed amount.


Blox: A software product that enables patients in hospitals to walk in and out without having to
do any paperwork. They click on a button and it checks them in, books an appointment with a vacant doctor and bills them throughout
as they move from one service post to the other. it saves and takes care of insurance and other payment information after first log in.

Start date: 10th October 2022 Projected Launch date: 1st December 2022 Team involved: same as the NoLines project with similar responsibilities.
